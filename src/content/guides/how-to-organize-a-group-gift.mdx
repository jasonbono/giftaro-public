> This snapshot includes a single example guide to demonstrate the MDX content
> system: frontmatter drives auto-routing, the sitemap, a per-page OpenGraph
> image, and Article JSON-LD. The full content library is omitted from the
> public snapshot.

Pick one real gift the recipient actually wants, set a target amount, and share
the link. Contributors chip in any amount from their phone, no account needed.

When the pool reaches its goal, funds settle to the organizer's connected bank
account and the organizer buys the gift. That is the whole loop.
